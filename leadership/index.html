As CEO of GreyOne Defense Inc., Rob Riegle brings decades of leadership experience spanning military operations, defense strategy, and advanced technology integration. His operational background across global conflict zones and high-stakes security environments provides the foundation for GreyOne Defense's mission-driven approach to modern defense challenges.

Under his leadership, GreyOne Defense has established itself as a trusted partner for government agencies and ministries of defense, delivering integrated solutions that bridge the gap between emerging technology and battlefield-ready execution. Rob's strategic vision drives the company's expansion across unmanned systems, intelligence operations, cyber capabilities, and defense manufacturing — ensuring GreyOne Defense remains at the forefront of the evolving global security landscape.
