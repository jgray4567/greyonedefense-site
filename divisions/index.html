Divisions

Specialized Expertise.
Unified Delivery.

Our divisions reflect the realities of modern security challenges: highly technical, rapidly evolving, and operationally interconnected. GreyOne Defense brings these capabilities together under a single platform.

01
Advanced Systems & UAV Division
This division focuses on unmanned aerial technologies, payload integration, ISR support, and counter-UAS capabilities. Designed for modern reconnaissance and force-protection requirements, the division supports both stand-alone deployment and broader integrated programs.
02
Intelligence & Cyber Operations Division
Focused on digital intelligence, mission-support analytics, cyber-enabled operations, and real-time situational awareness, this division helps clients identify, interpret, and act on complex data in operationally meaningful ways.
03
Weapons Systems & Manufacturing Division
This division supports defense manufacturing, systems integration, weapons-related program design, and platform adaptation. Its focus is on practical capability, operational durability, and alignment with end-user requirements.
04
Defense Logistics & Procurement Division
Responsible for procurement execution, sourcing strategy, delivery coordination, and regulatory navigation, this division is structured to support sensitive and demanding supply requirements at scale.
05
Strategic Advisory & Operational Support Division
This division provides planning, advisory, and field-oriented support for defense modernization, mission preparation, training, and operational alignment in complex environments.

A U.S.-based defense and intelligence company delivering integrated capabilities for governments and strategic partners worldwide.
